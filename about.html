The Journey So Far

It all started with a simple idea: to see what's beyond the next hill. Born in Bengal, the call of the mountains was always present. What began as weekend treks and borrowed cameras soon became a full-blown passion, a need to document not just the places, but the stories, the people, and the feelings of the road.

Explorer Shibaji isn't just a person, it's a brand born from that passion. It's about capturing the soul of travel and turning it into something you can hold, wear, and share.

  • 2015

    First solo trek to Sandakphu. The journey that started it all.

  • 2017

    Started filming journeys. The "Explorer Shibaji" YouTube channel was born.

  • 2020

    Birth of the Explorer brand. Launched the first T-shirt design.

  • 2024

    Reached 100k subscribers on YouTube and launched the first official store.

Mission & Vision

"To turn travel into wearable art and inspire Bengalis to explore more."
